FEATURES

    FC IQ: This is a comprehensive overhaul of the tactical system, allowing players to influence team strategies more effectively. It includes modernised team tactics and smarter AI for teammates, enabling a more dynamic gameplay experience. Players can now emulate the tactics of famous managers, adapting formations fluidly during matches.
    Player Roles: The game introduces distinct roles for players, enhancing how they fit into team strategies. This feature is designed to ensure that players are utilised effectively according to their strengths, making team composition more critical than ever.
    Goalkeeper PlayStyles: New gameplay mechanics for goalkeepers have been added, providing more depth to the position and allowing for varied styles of play.
    5v5 Rush Mode: Players can team up with friends in a new 5v5 mode, which adds a fresh competitive element to the game.
